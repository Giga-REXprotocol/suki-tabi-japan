Redesign places to be more user-friendly for people from different cultures
Wi-tabi refers to foreign tourists visiting Japan who come to Japan and face a number of internet and other problems, such as
``Wi-Fi and communication.''
We are working to solve these problems, make foreign audiences more enjoyable in Japan, and hope that they will come to Japan more often.

重新设计场所，对来自不同文化的人来说更加方便
Wi-tabi是指访问日本的外国游客，
来到日本后会遇到一些互联网和其他问题，例如“Wi-Fi和通讯”。
我们正在努力解决这些问题，让外国观众在日本享受更多乐趣，并希望他们更多地来日本。
