Redesign places to be more user-friendly for people from different cultures
Project overview
Places with poor multicultural coexistence
Tourist spots in Japan with insufficient public Wi-Fi environment
Redesign proposal
Wi-Fi service affiliated with tourist destinations
The ideal image of symbiosis we aim for
Allowing foreigners to safely tour Japan, which has a different culture for them.
→As a solution to this, we are planning a service that will enrich the Wi-Fi environment to make it easier to use smartphones, a tool that thins the barriers between different cultures.
